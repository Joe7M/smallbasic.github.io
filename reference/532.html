NOSOUND

NOSOUND

Home > Reference > Console

Stop background sound and clear the sound queue.

Example

sound 1000, 5000, 50 BG         ' 1000 Hz, 5000 ms, 50% volume, play in background
delay(1000)                     ' Wait 1s and then cancel sound
nosound
pause
