SmallBASIC File Transfer

Easy file transfer between your Android device and your desktop using WIFI.

File Transfer in the Browser

On your Android device:

  1. Enable WIFI on your mobile to access your home network.
  2. In the [setup]-screen, select a font, enable extensions and give a port number, for example 5432.
  3. Follow the instructions to restart SmallBASIC.
  4. Open the [about]-screen to display the device IP and token.

On your desktop browser:

  1. Enter http://device-ip:port. For example: http://192.168.1.5:5432.
  2. You should see a login screen with “Enter your access token”.
  3. Enter the token as shown in the [about]-page.
  4. You can now upload and download SmallBASIC files between your desktop and your mobile.

File Transfer in the IDE

On your Android device:

  1. Enable WIFI on your mobile to access your home network.
  2. In the [setup]-screen, select a font, enable extensions and give a port number, for example 5432.
  3. Follow the instructions to restart SmallBASIC.
  4. Open the [about]-screen to display the device IP and token.

On your desktop:

  1. Start the SDL or FLTK-version of SmallBASIC
  2. Navigate to the .bas file then click to begin editing.
  3. Press F3, then enter the address and port as shown in the Android [about]-screen (IP-Address:PORT), for example 192.168.1.5:5432
  4. Enter the token as shown in the [about]-page.
  5. Once the settings are successful, you can press F4 to upload your program.

On your Android device:

  1. If EDITOR [ON] in the three-dot menu, the uploaded program will be opened in the editor. If EDITOR [OFF], the uploaded program will be executed.

At first connection after restarting SmallBASIC on your Android device, you might need to give permission for web portal access.
